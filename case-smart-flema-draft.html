The idea arrived out of order.

Most projects start with a brief. Smart Flema started with a feeling — the uncomfortable kind, where you've just finished a project and you suspect you built the wrong thing for the wrong people.

I'd just wrapped the Smart City Lampang pilot. CCTV feeds, AI people-counting, LINE OA alerts for the municipality. Technically, it worked. But I kept noticing who wasn't in our stakeholder meetings: the people who actually made daily decisions in the spaces we were measuring.
